## What is SOLID?

The [SOLID Principles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SOLID) are five principles about how to
write code, laid out by software engineering  "Uncle Bob" in the early 2000s. The principles
are:

- Single responsibility principle
- Open-closed principle
- Liskov substituion principle
- Interface segregation principle
- Dependency inversion principle




make code more <u>understandable</u>, <u>flexible</u> and <u>maintainable</u>
